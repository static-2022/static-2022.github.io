      ABOUT
      Impact-X acts as a catalyst for innovation, breaking down barriers and investigating novel ideas. We want to contribute to a sustainable, just, and inclusive future with an emphasis on the Sustainable Development Goals of the United Nations. Come along with us on this revolutionary journey as technology acts as a force for good. Together, we can build a world where technology improves people's lives and spurs further development. Embrace Impact-X's boundless potential, disrupt the current quo, and unleash your ideas.

      Objectives

      • All

      Foster Innovation

      The main goal of Impact-X is to stimulate innovation by giving participants a space to think creatively, investigate novel concepts, and come up with unique solutions. We want to foster an atmosphere where participants are inspired to explore the limits of technology and produce ground-breaking answers to social problems.

      Drive Positive effect

      We are dedicated to using technology to create positive effect. Our goal is to enable participants to create solutions that address social, environmental, and economic problems in a real and enduring way. We urge participants to match their initiatives with the Sustainable Development Goals to make sure that their efforts advance a more just and sustainable future.

      Encourage and Empower

      ImpactX aims to encourage and embolden people to become changemakers. By giving them the tools, direction, and support they need to realize their ideas, we want to kindle in them a passion for utilizing technology to make a difference. We hope to motivate participants to continue their journey after the hackathon by displaying accomplished projects and stories of impact.

      F.A.Q

      • All

      Is this Hackathon an online event or offline?

      This is an offline event. 

      Is there any registration fee?

      Rs. 150/- For IEEE MEMBER and Rs.200/- For Non IEEE MEMBER

      Should you stay on campus overnight?

      Yes, we request all participants to stay on-campus during the entire hackthon to get the optimal experience.

      Can I participate without a team?

      No, this hackathon is an team event and can participate in teams of 3 to 5.

      What are the facilities arranged by the organizers?

      Food and accomodation are arranged by us.

      Will the hackathon has Wi-fi facilities?

      Yes absolutely, we'll provide Wi-fi Internet facilities.

      On Spot registration available ?

      No, you have to register before 11 Dec 23, 06:00 PM using the Registration Link.

      Can members from different college and branches form a team?

      Yes, absolutely we encourage you guys to be diverse and join teams from various backgrounds and collaborate.
